Enum BridgeAudioFormat

Namespace
CalloraVoipSdk
Assembly
CalloraVoipSdk.Client.dll

Audio format delivered to and expected from a media consumer (the "bridge"/tap), such as an AI realtime bridge. When it differs from the negotiated wire codec, the SDK transcodes audio transparently so the consumer sees a single, fixed codec regardless of what the SIP peer negotiated.

public enum BridgeAudioFormat

Fields

Passthrough = 0

Deliver and send the raw negotiated wire payload unchanged (default). The consumer must handle whatever codec was negotiated.

Pcmu = 1

Always deliver and accept G.711 µ-law (PCMU, 8 kHz). Inbound wire audio is transcoded to µ-law and outbound µ-law is transcoded to the wire codec. Supported wire codecs: Opus and G.711 (µ-law/A-law); other codecs are delivered as passthrough with a warning.
